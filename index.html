About

Food and culture links the globe. Byte Sized is a website for all foodies, chefs, and all types of people to appreciate dishes and cuisine from around the world. We hope to provide a safe and fun environment for all to learn recipes and form cultural connections from various countries. Click the recipes tab to see some of our favorite recipes from various countries and use the contact form to submit/suggest your own! If you're looking for a fun food related adventure, check out the activities tab!
